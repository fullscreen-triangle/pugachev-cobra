# Asset slots — leave-before-you-arrive

Drop source clips here. Filenames must match exactly.

| File | Content | Duration needed | Notes |
|------|---------|----------------|-------|
| `parkour.mp4` | Continuous parkour run | ≥ 8s | Prefer tracking shot, no cuts to stillness. Body always in motion, routing *around* obstacles, never entering through doors. |
| `longjump.mp4` | Long jump — Beamon (1968 Mexico) or Powell (1991 Tokyo) | ≥ 9s | Full arc: approach + flight + landing. The instrument-exceeding distance is the point. |
| `cobra.mp4` | Pugachev Cobra manoeuvre | ≥ 8s | Must include the nose-peak frame (~120° AoA). Side-on or front-quarter angle works best. The SAAB Draken GIF in `/assets/img/` is a reference frame. |

## Timing notes

- The glitch fires at **19s–20s** relative to composition start, which is 2s into the cobra clip.
  Trim your cobra clip so the nose-peak frame lands around the 2s mark.
- The main line ("leave before you arrive") appears at **20s–24s**.
  The cobra should still be visible and holding or recovering through this window.
- B&W lifts at 17s when the cobra clip begins. If your cobra clip has a distinctive
  colour signature (afterburner, sky blue), that contrast does the work automatically.
